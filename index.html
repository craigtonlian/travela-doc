About

Want to plan a trip with your friends, but you guys just can't decide on what places to visit? Or perhaps you guys cannot seem to find a way to track your expenses on your trip? Or maybe you guys just cannot seem to find the right time to come together to throughly plan out the trip?

Well, fear no more, for travela is here!

Our app allows you to quickly and seamlessly collaborate with your friends to plan out your trip in detail, from minutae such as how to obtain the local public transport card, to the big ticket items such as your plane tickets and hotel bookings.

It also allows you to track your expenses by setting trip-specific budgets, eliminating the mental gymnastics needed to calculate the amount of money one owes another in the group; it is all in the app!

So, what are you waiting for? Download the app now and start your next great adventure!

Features

Some of the features of the app include:

  • User Authentication and Customisation
    • Log in securely with Firebase Authentication, using your favourite and trusted Accounts from Google, Apple and Facebook
    • Seamlessly collaborate with other users of the app in real-time
    • Customise your profile to make the experience uniquely yours
  • Document Organisation
    • Upload your important travel documents and access them on the go
    • Label documents and group documents up into folders for greater organisation of your most important travel documents
    • Protect your most sensitive data by encrypting your data with a password or with your device's biometric scanners
  • Expenditure Tracking
    • Set trip budgets and set alerts and reminders to stay on budget
    • Track expenses for your trips by creating expense entries for said trip
    • Visualise your expenses with easy-to-understand charts and graphs
  • Itinerary Planning
    • Location-based suggestions on possible places of interest depending on the location of your trip
    • Discuss and share potential places of interest to visit for future trips with Idea Boards
    • Plan out your trip in detail with your loved ones
    • Integration with Google Maps to visualise the routes to take while on your trip
  • Weather Forecasting
    • Find out more about the weather conditions at your destination to allow for better planning

To find out more, click here to go to the Wiki where more information about the design choices and documentation of the features are located at!

Alternatively, you may navigate to the folders nested within lib and read the README.md files for more information about the files and classes within the folder!

Install

Ensure that you have Flutter installed on your device, and have downloaded a Windows, MacOS, iOS or Android emulator if you wish to run the app on any of the aforementioned operating systems.

Click here to find out how to install Flutter on your device (do note that Dart is automatically installed when you install the Flutter SDK through the link above; you DO NOT need to install Dart manually using this method).

We are currently using Flutter 3.10.1 (with Dart 3.0.1 included) on our development machines. We also tested the app on an iOS and Android emulator on both Windows and MacOS. Your mileage may vary should you choose to use an older version of flutter in the app.

Usage

Click here to find out how to set up your favourite IDE or code editor for use with Flutter and Dart.

To run the app, simply navigate to lib/main.dart and run the file. If successful, the app should compile and be properly emulated in your emulator of choice.

Do note that you should only run lib/main.dart as it is the entry point to the application. Should you decide to use any other Dart files as the entry point to the application, errors may occur.
